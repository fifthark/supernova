Pennant League — Advanced

Starts Monday 20 April · ACM Sports Centre, Truganina · Weekly · Ongoing

What it is

A structured weekly doubles league for advanced players. You get drafted into a balanced team, compete against the same pool of teams each week, and build momentum across a proper season — not a one-off hit.

Who it's for

  • Advanced players wanting consistent weekly competition
  • Players done with disorganised socials and mismatched games
  • Anyone ready to commit to a season, not just a session

Format

Weekly doubles Balanced team drafts Graded competition Live standings Semis & Finals

What's included

  • Courts & shuttles provided
  • Live standings & match stats
  • Weekly match play — same night, same venue
  • Season-ending Finals & awards

Advanced level only. Not sure if this is the right grade? Register interest via the app — we'll confirm your grade placement before the season locks.

Squad Tournament

Saturday 3 May · Badminton Connect, Noble Park · Registration closes 22 April

What it is

A one-day team event built around squads of four — 2 male, 2 female. You compete together across the day, backing your teammates from the sideline between your own matches. One win from anyone swings the whole tie.

Match format

  • 4 games per tie: 1 MD · 1 WD · 2 XD
  • 30-point games, no deuce
  • 75-minute cap per tie
  • Knockouts: 21-point games
  • Round robin pools → knockout finals

Entry & prizes

$40 per player

$200 prize pool · Graded competition · Play at your level

Grades available

Beginner Intermediate Advanced

Select your grade on registration. Admin confirms placement after entries close.
