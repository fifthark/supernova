No more scrambling for games. No more mismatched opponents. Just structured doubles — drafted teams, live standings, and knockouts worth playing for. Advanced league running now at ACM Truganina.

8 Week competitive season including Semis & Finals
4 Players per team, drafted by skill level
2.5h Per week — same night, same venue, every round
Live Standings, match stats & ratings updated after every game

Step 1

Register

Sign up via the app. No obligation until teams confirmed.

Step 2

Assess

Attend assessment day. Get accurately rated.

Step 3

Draft

Placed into a balanced team of 4.

Rounds 1–6

Regular Season

Round robin. Every team plays every other team once.

Week 7

Semi-finals

Top 4 teams battle it out.

Week 8

Grand Final

Final + Trophy Night. Wins are earned.

Weekly format

  • 7:00 PM arrival — structured warm-up
  • Team vs team — 12 matches per night
  • 3 courts in use, 1 team on bye each round
  • Every match recorded, live standings updated
  • Approximately 2.5 hours start to finish

What's included

  • Courts & shuttles every week
  • Live standings & match stats
  • Weekly MVP recognition
  • Personal end-of-season stats report
  • Semis, Finals + Trophy Night

Common questions

What if I miss a week?

We'll arrange a fill-in so your team isn't short. You'll receive a social session credit valid for 3 months at any SuperNova session.

How are teams balanced?

Every player is assessed before the season. Teams are drafted by skill and reviewed in the first few weeks — adjustments made to protect competitive balance.

What level do I need to be?

The Advanced league is for strong competitive players. Register interest and we'll guide you on the right grade — no pressure.

Can I join without a full season commitment?

Yes — the Reserve Pool is free and flexible. You fill in when teams need cover, no season commitment required.
